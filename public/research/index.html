Research

I'm interested in consumer behavior and behavioral economics in tourism and hospitality, particularly how individuals make decisions under different market conditions. I also study tourism economics and examine how strategy, finance, and technological innovation shape the industry. My work combines econometrics and data analytics to explore these questions.
