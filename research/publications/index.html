The information characteristics of YouTube tourism content and their impacts on user satisfaction and intention to visit and share information: the moderating role of word-of-mouth information acceptance

This study explores how characteristics of YouTube tourism content influence user satisfaction, and how satisfaction, in turn, affects viewers’ intention to visit a destination and share information with others. Surveyed 244 viewers of YouTube tourism videos Found that usefulness and entertainment value positively affected user satisfaction User satisfaction positively influenced both intention to visit and intention to share Intention to visit also positively influenced intention to share The relationship between satisfaction and sharing was moderated by word-of-mouth information acceptance 📄 Download Full Paper (PDF)
