About

I am a PhD candidate in Hospitality and Tourism Management at Virginia Tech (Pamplin College of Business), advised by Professor Juan Luis Nicolau. My research integrates behavioral economics, machine learning, and econometric modeling to study tourist decision-making, demand forecasting, and the role of digital culture in shaping travel behavior.

Before academia, I spent six years at Samsung Electronics as a B2B technical engineer, working with global clients including Intel, AMD, Dell, and Amazon — an experience that grounds my research in real-world managerial contexts.
