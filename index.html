Hyojun Jung

PhD Candidate · Hospitality & Tourism Management · Virginia Tech

I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Hospitality and Tourism Management at Virginia Tech (Pamplin College of Business), advised by Professor Juan Luis Nicolau. My research focuses on tourism demand forecasting, consumer behavior, and data analytics in hospitality and tourism — with an emphasis on integrating machine learning and econometric methods.

Prior to academia, I worked as an engineer at Samsung Electronics (2015–2021), leading B2B technical marketing for DRAM memory products with global partners including Intel, AMD, Dell, and Amazon.
